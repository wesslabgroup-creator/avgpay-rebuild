Salary Negotiation Templates (AvgPay)

Friendly Email
Subject: Offer discussion
Hi [Name],
Thank you for the offer. I'm excited about the role and team.
Based on scope and market benchmarks, I'd like to discuss a package in the [X-Y] range.
Would you be open to a quick call to review options?

Firm Email
Subject: Compensation alignment
Hi [Name],
I'm aligned with the role and timeline. To accept confidently, I need total compensation to align with market for this scope.
I'm targeting [X-Y], and I can sign quickly if we can close that gap.

Direct Email
Subject: Ready to close
Hi [Name],
I'm ready to move forward. If we can bring base to [X] and equity to [Y], I'll accept today.

Call Prompts
Opening: I'm excited to join and would like to review compensation details before final acceptance.
Anchor: Given scope and market context, I'm targeting [X-Y] total compensation.
Close: Great, what is the process and timeline for an updated package?

Counter Responses
1) I appreciate the update. If we can adjust [component] by [amount], I'm ready to close.
2) Could we improve sign-on or equity to bridge the remaining gap?
3) If level is fixed, can we review scope/title alignment for the first review cycle?
